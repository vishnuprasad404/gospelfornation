Sacraments

01

Water Baptism

All those born again believers, by accepting Christ in their hearts as Saviour must accept the water immersion Baptism, the commandment of our LORD. It is a public witness of the believer that he/she is baptized in to His death, buried with Him, raised from the dead, as Christ was raised to live in the newness of life. (Mathew 28:19, Acts 10:47, Rom. 6:4).
