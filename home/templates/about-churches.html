About Churches

In 2009, Pastor Finny John and family were used as instruments by God for laying the foundations of a prayer gathering in High Wycombe, Bucks. The gathered believers prayed for the needy and the suffering. It was initially a service focussed at the malayalee congregation. After initial delays in the addition of members to the congregation, God opened ways where new families relocated to High Wycombe.

Wycombe Prayer Centre  

High Wycombe, often referred to as Wycombe, is a large market town in Buckinghamshire, England and one-time 'Furniture Capital of England'. High Wycombe is a lively, modern location with plenty of tourist attractions & points of interest.

Based in this beautiful town is Wycombe Prayer centre, a Malayalee Pentecostal family church of believers who love the Lord and seek to fulfil his purposes as the Lord leads in any given life setting. Spirit-filled worship, sound teaching of God’s Word and prayer are central to the Wycombe Prayer Centre community. We wholeheartedly welcome you to our regular services.

Oxford Prayer Center

God emancipated humankind from the bondage of sin and slavery. His ultimate purpose of redemption is indeed for us to dwell in his presence, enjoy his love and propagate His word. It gives us great joy and immense pleasure to then proclaim that the Oxford Prayer Centre is by all measure a place to enjoy and experience God's presence in the heart of Oxford. Oxford Prayer Centre is a Malayalee Pentecostal church worshiping the Lord in truth and spirit. The gates of this prayer home are also open to the underprivileged and needy, we hope to support them in prayers. We are commissioned to live and face the tomorrow in humility and holiness.

Under the Lordship of Christ, we as a church hope to pray for the healing of boroughs, restoration of communities and reconciliation of nations across with a desire of eventually witnessing a wind of revival and transformation..

Aylesbury Prayer Centre

Aylesbury is a bustling market town located just north of the Chiltern Hills, Buckinghamshire, in the rich agricultural area known as the Vale of Aylesbury. Aylesbury being the birthplace of the Paralympics does hold a significant place in history.

We have regular meetings based in Aylesbury. These include area meetings and regular systematic Bible studies. Please do get in touch for fellowship and further details. Contact Details
