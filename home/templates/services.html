Services

Sunday School

“Train up a child in the way he should go, And when he is old he will not depart from it.” [Proverbs 22:6.]

We strongly believe that the church has a huge role in developing the next generation. To provide the knowledge of Christ and biblical truths, we organise weekly Sunday school services. These Sunday schools are run on the first three Sunday mornings from 9am to 10am at Scouts Hall, Keep Hill Road, High Wycombe. The Sunday school comprises introductions, prayers, thought for the day, learning songs, completion of assignments, presentations, testimonies and also age appropriate classes. The Sunday school sessions are being run by members of the church who have been DBS checked. The Sunday school classes also provide opportunity for students to clear their doubts and seek help in spiritual matter. We also provide counselling and career support. Annual examinations are also run based on the curriculum that is being followed.
